Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?

Your game progress is stored locally on your device. Unfortunately, if you delete the app or switch devices, your progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your streaks and statistics.

How does the daily challenge work?

A new puzzle is available every day. Complete it to build your streak! If you miss a day, you can watch a short video to unlock that day's puzzle and continue your streak.

How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on the puzzle difficulty and how quickly you complete it. Harder puzzles and faster times earn more points. Daily challenges and maintaining streaks also give bonus points!

Can I play offline?

Yes! Sudoku Owl works completely offline. You only need an internet connection to watch ads for unlocking missed daily challenges.

How do I turn on Night Mode?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and toggle "Night Mode" on. This enables a darker theme that's easier on your eyes.

What do the hints do?

Hints reveal the correct number for a selected cell. Use them wisely - they're there to help when you're truly stuck!
